Classic Potato Salad

Creamy, Tangy & Crunchy!

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs russet potatoes, peeled
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 teaspoons kosher salt, or to taste, divided
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 5 stalks green onions, sliced (white and green parts)
  • 2 stalks celery, minced
  • ½ cup chopped spicy sweed dill pickles (such as Wickles)
  • ⅓ cup sour cream
  • 3 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 2 teaspoons pickle juice
  • ¾ ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground paprika
  • ¼ cup sliced green olives with pimientos

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into 1 1/2-inch cubes.
  2. Place in a large pot and cover with water by 2 inches.
  3. Add eggs and 3 teaspoons salt.
  4. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  5. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 9 minutes.
  6. Remove eggs with a slotted spoon and immediately immerse in ice water.
  7. Continue cooking potatoes until fork-tender.
  8. Peel and chop the eggs.
  9. Add mayonnaise, onions, celery, pickles, sour cream, mustard, pickle juice, dill, and pepper to a large bowl
  10. Stir to combine.
  11. Stir in potatoes and eggs until well blended, mashing potatoes very slightly.
  12. Season with salt and pepper, adding up to 1 teaspoon more salt.
  13. Transfer to a serving bowl, sprinkle with paprika, and garnish with olives.
  14. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours or overnight.
